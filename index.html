ABOUT OPEN HOUSE:

Open house is a unique platform initiated by VNRVJIET. It emphasizes on showcasing the innovative ideas of the students and faculty of the college. Through Open House we encourage young minds to express their thoughts in the form of project demonstrations and poster presentations. These would include various domains of engineering and multidisciplinary projects.
